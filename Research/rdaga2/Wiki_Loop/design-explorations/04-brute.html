QUANTUM MECHANICS is the fundamental physical theory that describes the behaviour of nature at atomic and subatomic scales. It departs from classical mechanics in positing that energy, momentum and other physical quantities are often restricted to discrete values; that objects have characteristics of both particles and waves; and that there are limits to the precision with which conjugate quantities can be known prior to measurement.
