> quantum mechanics is the fundamental physical theory that describes the behaviour of nature at atomic and subatomic scales. it departs from classical mechanics in positing that energy, momentum and other physical quantities are often restricted to discrete values.

> objects exhibit characteristics of both particles and waves (wave-particle duality); there exist limits to the accuracy with which quantities can be predicted prior to measurement, given a complete set of initial conditions (uncertainty principle).
