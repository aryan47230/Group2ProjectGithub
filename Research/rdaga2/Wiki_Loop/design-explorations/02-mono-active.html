Neural
network.

CATEGORY · Computer science ORIGIN · 1943 REFS · 84 DEPTH · LVL. 03

A neural network is a computational model inspired by the structure and function of biological neural networks in the brain. It consists of interconnected nodes — or neurons — arranged in layers that transform inputs through weighted connections.

Modern deep networks learn by propagating error backwards through these weights, and now underpin most of machine perception, language, and generative work.
